Coin Dispenser

(Internet Of Things, Electronics, Arduino, C Programming)

Welcome to our currency dispenser project, designed to efficiently dispense the desired number of coins based on the input amount. Utilizing an Arduino Uno R3 and servo motors, our project dispenses five rupee coins from one pipe and two and one rupee coins from another. Initially aiming for a coin sorting and counting machine, we pivoted due to challenges with coin radii. With input provided through the serial monitor, our dispenser accurately dispenses the required coins. Hardware setup and Arduino connections were completed, followed by coding and design efforts. Our project was fully functional and well-received, earning us the first prize in the EAC section and a Raspberry Pi.
